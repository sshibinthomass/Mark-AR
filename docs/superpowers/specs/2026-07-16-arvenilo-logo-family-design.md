# Arvenilo Logo Family Design

## Goal

Create a scalable logo family based on the selected Arvenilo Spatial Aperture concept. The system must support:

1. the Arvenilo parent company;
2. four company category and product-platform brands;
3. five separately marketable AnchorAR solution apps;
4. future category brands and AnchorAR apps without redesigning the identity.

The generated work is a polished visual identity concept. Production vector construction and formal trademark clearance remain separate finalization steps.

## Selected Master Direction

The selected Arvenilo master mark is the Spatial Aperture concept supplied by the founder.

Its permanent visual DNA is:

- a bold, symmetrical outer letter A;
- an open central aperture;
- a narrow vertical opening at the apex;
- an illuminated signal point;
- a lower mint reality plane;
- a Spatial Ink, Signal Mint, Reality Mist, and Anchor Gold palette;
- a geometric uppercase `ARVENILO` wordmark.

The outer A silhouette is the primary recognition asset. It must not be redesigned for individual categories or products.

## Brand Architecture

```text
Arvenilo
├── Arvenilo Agents
├── Arvenilo Spatial
├── Arvenilo Network
└── AnchorAR by Arvenilo
    ├── AnchorAR Books
    ├── AnchorAR Menu
    ├── AnchorAR Cards
    ├── AnchorAR Ads
    └── AnchorAR Stories
```

### Roles

- **Arvenilo** is the parent company and master brand.
- **Arvenilo Agents** represents Agentic AI capabilities.
- **Arvenilo Spatial** represents XR and spatial-computing capabilities.
- **Arvenilo Network** represents trusted Web3 and decentralized infrastructure.
- **AnchorAR by Arvenilo** is the active product platform.
- **AnchorAR solution apps** are separately marketable products that share the AnchorAR platform, account system, and technical foundation.

## Recommended Construction: Locked Aperture + Solution Glyph

The family will use three levels of visual inheritance.

### Level 1: Arvenilo master mark

The selected Spatial Aperture symbol remains unchanged.

The master composition contains:

- Spatial Ink outer A;
- white central aperture;
- Anchor Gold signal point;
- Signal Mint lower reality plane.

### Level 2: Category and platform marks

Every Level 2 mark preserves:

- the exact outer A silhouette;
- the apex opening;
- the overall proportions;
- the Arvenilo wordmark family;
- the central-aperture placement.

Only the inner signal system changes.

#### Arvenilo Agents

- one Digital Violet intelligent node;
- one short directional route or handoff path;
- meaning: autonomous action, orchestration, and decision-making.

#### Arvenilo Spatial

- two restrained mint spatial layers or depth planes;
- one Anchor Gold focus point;
- meaning: spatial presence, environments, and immersive interaction.

#### Arvenilo Network

- exactly three small connected nodes;
- controlled mint, violet, and gold accents;
- meaning: trusted connection, portability, and interoperability.

#### AnchorAR by Arvenilo

- the selected mint reality plane remains prominent;
- the Anchor Gold point becomes a precise anchor, registration point, or crosshair;
- meaning: digital experiences attached to images, products, and places.

The Level 2 symbols must remain recognizable in one colour. Accent colours support meaning but cannot be structurally necessary.

## Level 3: AnchorAR Solution Apps

Each AnchorAR solution app inherits the AnchorAR platform symbol. The outer A, apex opening, central aperture, and gold anchor point remain fixed.

The lower mint reality plane becomes a standardized solution-glyph zone. The app-specific glyph occupies this zone without touching or distorting the outer A.

### App glyphs

#### AnchorAR Books

- simplified open-book glyph;
- no page text or education clichés such as graduation caps;
- communicates learning content and interactive books.

#### AnchorAR Menu

- simplified menu panel with three short horizontal rows;
- no fork-and-knife or chef-hat cliché;
- communicates restaurant menus and item exploration.

#### AnchorAR Cards

- two offset rectangular cards;
- no playing-card suits;
- communicates business cards, invitations, greeting cards, and collectible interactive cards.

#### AnchorAR Ads

- one compact broadcast wedge with two restrained signal lines;
- avoid a loud literal megaphone where possible;
- communicates campaigns, promotions, and interactive advertisements.

#### AnchorAR Stories

- open-page or spread glyph combined with one restrained speech-frame corner;
- avoid comic-book explosion shapes;
- communicates comics, magazines, editorial content, and visual storytelling.

### App icon rule

At small sizes, the app icon contains only:

- the AnchorAR-derived symbol;
- the app glyph;
- the shared dark rounded-square container when an operating-system icon is required.

The full app name never appears inside the app icon.

## Naming and Lockups

### Parent and category lockups

- `ARVENILO`
- `ARVENILO AGENTS`
- `ARVENILO SPATIAL`
- `ARVENILO NETWORK`

The category descriptor is visually subordinate to `ARVENILO`.

### Product-platform lockup

Primary public form:

> `AnchorAR`

Formal endorsed form:

> `AnchorAR by Arvenilo`

The endorsed form is used on websites, presentations, legal pages, launch material, and first-touch communications. The shorter form is permitted in compact product interfaces after the relationship is established.

### Solution-app lockups

- `AnchorAR Books`
- `AnchorAR Menu`
- `AnchorAR Cards`
- `AnchorAR Ads`
- `AnchorAR Stories`

`AnchorAR` remains visually consistent across every app. The solution descriptor uses one shared weight, size, spacing, and alignment rule.

## Colour System

### Core colours

- Spatial Ink: `#081D21`
- Signal Mint: `#5EEAD4`
- Reality Mist: `#F4FBFA`
- Digital Violet: `#7456F1`
- Anchor Gold: `#F4B942`

### Colour hierarchy

- Spatial Ink defines the permanent outer silhouette and wordmark.
- Signal Mint represents reality layers, active experiences, and accessible interaction.
- Anchor Gold identifies focus, placement, and anchored context.
- Digital Violet is reserved for autonomous intelligence and selected network signals.

AnchorAR solution apps do not receive unrelated rainbow colours. They are differentiated primarily by glyph shape.

## Future Expansion Rule

A future AnchorAR app can be added when it has:

1. a distinct customer need;
2. a separately marketable offer or landing page;
3. a simple noun descriptor;
4. a recognizable glyph that works inside the solution-glyph zone;
5. no need to alter the outer A or AnchorAR core.

Future app names should follow:

> `AnchorAR + simple noun`

Examples of valid future patterns include `AnchorAR Retail`, `AnchorAR Places`, or `AnchorAR Events`. These examples are naming patterns, not announced products.

## ImageGen Production Strategy

The selected founder image is the visual reference for all generations.

ImageGen 2 will create separate concept assets for:

1. Arvenilo master-logo refinement;
2. Arvenilo Agents;
3. Arvenilo Spatial;
4. Arvenilo Network;
5. AnchorAR by Arvenilo;
6. AnchorAR Books;
7. AnchorAR Menu;
8. AnchorAR Cards;
9. AnchorAR Ads;
10. AnchorAR Stories.

Each generation must preserve the selected outer A and change only the approved inner system. Every concept will show:

- primary symbol;
- horizontal lockup;
- monochrome preview;
- app-icon or small-size preview.

If text rendering is unreliable, ImageGen will be used for symbols only and exact lockup text will be composed in Canva.

## Canva Review Presentation

The editable Canva review presentation will use the Arvenilo palette and contain:

1. title and selected master direction;
2. three-level brand architecture;
3. Arvenilo master mark;
4. Level 2 category and platform marks;
5. AnchorAR platform construction;
6. five AnchorAR solution apps;
7. monochrome and small-size comparison;
8. future-expansion rule and next-step selection.

Canva assembly depends on access to a connected Canva account and an available brand kit. If no Arvenilo brand kit exists, the documented palette and typography direction will be used as the presentation brief.

## Visual Constraints

- no change to the selected outer A silhouette;
- no gradients, glow, bevels, glass, or photorealistic mockups in the logo artwork;
- no detailed illustrations inside the aperture;
- no more than three small nodes in any symbol;
- no tiny text inside app icons;
- no category-specific rainbow palette;
- no generic blockchain, brain, cube, infinity, sparkle, shield, globe, or circuit-board symbols;
- no unapproved claims that future Arvenilo category brands are currently launched.

## Acceptance Criteria

The logo family is ready for founder review when:

- all ten required marks are represented;
- the selected master silhouette remains visibly unchanged;
- category marks differ through inner signal systems only;
- AnchorAR app marks visibly belong to one family;
- every app glyph is recognizable without its label;
- all marks have credible monochrome versions;
- all marks remain understandable at app-icon size;
- every lockup uses the exact approved name;
- the system can accept future AnchorAR apps without restructuring;
- the review presentation clearly distinguishes parent brand, category brands, platform, and solution apps.

## Deferred Finalization

After the founder selects and approves the family:

- redraw the chosen marks as exact production SVG geometry;
- create transparent PNG exports;
- define clear space and minimum sizes;
- create light, dark, and monochrome masters;
- perform exact and similarity-based trademark searches;
- update the Arvenilo brand book with the final logo family;
- create final Canva templates and brand-kit assets.
